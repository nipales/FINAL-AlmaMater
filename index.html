High School Activities and Events

UB High believes in the holistic development of the youth. It offers a regular High School program and also a Special Program for the Arts and Sciences (SPAS) which caters to the different and varied interests of the future young professionals like you.

  • Public Speaking
  • Arnis
  • Social Link
  • Table Tennis
  • Chess
  • Taekwondo
  • Math (Varsity Team)
  • Athletics
  • Basketball
  • Swimming
